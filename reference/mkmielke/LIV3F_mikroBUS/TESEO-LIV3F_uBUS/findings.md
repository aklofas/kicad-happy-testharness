## FND-00000811: SAW RF filter F1 (B39162B4327P810) classified as 'fuse' type; All 4 I2C bus detections are false positives; BGA725L6 LNA has GND listed as a power rail in ic_power_rails; LC filter network for GPS ...

- **Status**: promoted
- **Analyzer**: schematic
- **Source**: TESEO-LIV3F_uBUS.sch.json
- **Created**: 2026-03-23

### Correct
- The analyzer found L1 (56nH) and L2 (6.8nH) with C4 (120pF) and C1 (56pF) forming LC matching networks resonating near GPS L1 band (1575.42 MHz). While exact frequencies don't match L1 band (this is an impedance matching network, not a resonant filter), the components are correctly identified as forming LC networks.
- D1 with value D_TVS_ALT and MPN ESDARF02-1BU2CK correctly identified as a TVS clamp protecting the RF input net from ESD. Protected net and clamp net correctly reported.

### Incorrect
- F1 has value=B4327, footprint=Filter_1411-5, description='FILTER SAW 5SMD', MPN=B39162B4327P810 — clearly a SAW bandpass filter for GPS L1. The analyzer classified it as 'fuse' because of the F reference designator prefix. This is a reference-designator-driven misclassification.
  (signal_analysis)
- The analyzer reports 4 I2C bus entries: net SDA connects to U3 pin 4 (1PPS output), net SCL connects to U3 pin 18 (RES2 reserved pin), net PPS connects to U3 pin 16 (named SDA in symbol), and an unnamed net to U3 RES2. None are actual I2C signals. The true I2C lines (if used) are not exposed on this mikroBUS adapter. KiCad 5 legacy schematic net names caused confusion.
  (signal_analysis)
- design_analysis.power_domains.ic_power_rails for U2 (BGA725L6 GPS LNA) lists power_rails=['GND', 'VCC_RF']. GND is a ground reference, not a power supply rail. Only VCC_RF should appear as the power supply. This is a domain grouping error.
  (signal_analysis)

- **Analyzer**: gerber
